My Resume

Summary

Magdalena Mishkov

I'm an aspiring designer based in Germany, originally from North Macedonia :)

Being passionate about creating memorable experiences and helping people by finding the best solution to them, was something that I enjoyed the most in my first job as a bank advisor. However, I was always in love with design and I wanted to use my creativity, so I’ve decided to combine all my acquired skills to make people's life a little bit easier and I dove in the UX/UI field.

Today, my main goal is translating concepts into visuals by paying close attention to details and converting it into highly interactive experiences always having in mind that: “The trend of design toward simplicity and accessibility in software happened for a reason—simple sells, simple’s usable and simple scales.” — Rand Fishkin.

I am looking for opportunities that will allow me to keep on learning, growing, and challenging myself. In my ideal working environment I expect transparency, mutual respect and open mindedness, which are core values that I hold in high regard.
